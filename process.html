Our Process

A simple workflow built to help veterans prepare stronger, more organized claims.

1. Schedule

Book your consultation through Calendly.

2. Intake

Complete the client intake form with your service history, claim type, and available records.

3. Review

We review your background, medical records, evidence gaps, and claim goals.

4. Strategy

You receive a clear roadmap for building a stronger claim package.

What You Receive

Depending on your service tier, you may receive a claim strategy roadmap, evidence checklist, medical record findings, recommended next steps, and pre-submission preparation guidance.
