Services & Claim Lanes

Our consulting services are structured around the primary VA claim lanes: New Claims, Supplemental Claims, and Appeals.

New Claims / Initial Claims

Intended for veterans initiating their first VA disability claim, as well as those submitting claims for new conditions that have not been previously claimed or evaluated by the VA.

$400 – $700

  • Full medical records review
  • Identification of claimable conditions
  • Fully Developed Claim strategy
  • Evidence gap analysis
  • Pre-submission audit

Supplemental Claims

Intended for veterans seeking to revisit a previously adjudicated VA claim after the one-year appeal period has expired.

This lane focuses on developing and organizing new and relevant evidence to support reconsideration of the prior decision.

$250 – $400

  • Prior decision review
  • Denial analysis
  • New and relevant evidence strategy
  • Nexus guidance
  • Claim restructuring guidance

Appeals

Intended for veterans who have received a VA decision and are still within the one-year appeal period.

This lane provides strategic guidance on available review options, including Higher-Level Review and Board Appeal pathways.

$500 – $1,200

  • Advanced case strategy
  • Appeal lane guidance
  • Evidence and argument structuring
  • Hearing preparation guidance
  • Highest-complexity consultation tier

We’ll help you identify the appropriate path during your consultation.
