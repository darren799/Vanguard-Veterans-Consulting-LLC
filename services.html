Services & Claim Lanes

Our consulting services are structured around the three VA claim lanes: New Claims, Supplemental Claims, and Appeals.

New Claims (Initial Claims)

For veterans who have never filed a VA disability claim before.

$400 – $700

  • Full medical records review
  • Identification of claimable conditions
  • Fully Developed Claim (FDC) strategy
  • Evidence gap analysis
  • Pre-submission audit

Supplemental Claims

For claims that have already been decided by the VA and are now outside of the one-year appeal window.

Requires submission of new and relevant evidence.

$250 – $400

  • Prior decision review
  • Denial analysis
  • New and relevant evidence strategy
  • Nexus guidance
  • Claim restructuring

Appeals

For claims that have already been decided and are still within the one-year appeal period.

Includes guidance on Higher-Level Review and Board Appeal options.

$500 – $1,200

  • Advanced case strategy
  • Appeal lane guidance (HLR / Board)
  • Evidence and argument structuring
  • Hearing preparation guidance
  • Highest-complexity consultation tier

Not Sure Which Lane You Fall Into?

We’ll help you identify the correct path during your consultation.
