About Us

Vanguard Veterans Consulting is a service-connected veteran owned and operated business. We help veterans understand the VA claim process with a focus on education, strategy, medical records review, evidence development, and fully developed claim preparation support. We guide veterans through the process while keeping the veteran in control of filing their own claim.
