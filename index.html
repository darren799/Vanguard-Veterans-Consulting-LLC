Frequently Asked Questions

Do you file VA claims for veterans?

No. We provide consulting and educational services only. Veterans are responsible for submitting their own claims to the VA.

How do I know which claim lane I fall into?

During your consultation, we determine whether your situation falls under a new claim, supplemental claim, or appeal based on your prior VA decisions and timelines.

What is a Fully Developed Claim (FDC)?

A Fully Developed Claim is a claim submitted with all required evidence upfront, which can help reduce delays and improve processing efficiency.

Do I need medical records before booking?

No. However, having access to your records allows for a more detailed consultation and strategy review.

What happens after I schedule a consultation?

After booking, you will complete an intake form. This allows us to review your situation before your consultation so we can provide targeted guidance.

Can you guarantee my claim will be approved?

We work diligently to help you build a comprehensive, well-supported claim. While outcomes cannot be guaranteed, we focus on strengthening your case through clear strategy and solid, verifiable evidence—such as official records, consistent documentation, photos or videos, witness statements, expert opinions, and key correspondence that can stand up to review.
